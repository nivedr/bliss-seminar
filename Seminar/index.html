BLISS Seminar

About

The BLISS seminar (formerly NCD seminar) is the area seminar of the Berkeley Laboratory for Information and System Sciences. Talks at the seminar cover topics including but not limited to information and coding theory, signal processing, optimization, statistics, and control.

The list of talks for the current semester can be found below, and past seminars from 2016 onwards are listed here. For an archive of all talks from 1996-2015, visit the old webpage.

Feel free to add a Google Calendar of our talks to your calendar.

To subscribe to our mailing list, click here.

To give a talk at the seminar, contact Nived Rajaraman or Tom Courtade.
